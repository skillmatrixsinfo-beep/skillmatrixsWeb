Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? Here are answers to the queries we get most often about courses, batches, payments and placements.

How long are your courses?

Our core bootcamps are 45 days with daily instructor sessions and project work. Advanced modules extend beyond 45 days for topics like AI integrations and Spring Boot microservices.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes — resume reviews, mock interviews and hiring partner introductions are part of our placement support. Specific placement guarantees depend on the program package — contact admissions for details.

What are the payment options?

We accept UPI (GPay/PhonePe/Paytm), bank transfers, and online payments. EMI options are available for selected programs — request an invoice for details.

Do I need prior experience?

Basic HTML/CSS/JS is helpful for fast-track bootcamps. We also offer foundation sessions for beginners to bring them up to speed before core lessons.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes — we provide course completion certificates for participants who complete project and assessment requirements.

Where are your offline classes held?

We run classroom cohorts in Miyapur, Chandanagar and Kukatpally. Exact venue details are provided on registration & the contact page.
