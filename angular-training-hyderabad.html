Why Learn Angular?

Angular is widely used in enterprise web applications for its stability, TypeScript-first approach, dependency injection and robust CLI. Companies look for developers who can build maintainable, testable frontends — skills you’ll get through our practical modules and projects.

What you will learn

  • Fundamentals: TypeScript, Components, Templates, Data binding
  • Advanced: RxJS, Observables, state management patterns
  • Forms: Template-driven & Reactive forms, validation
  • Services & DI: Building reusable services and dependency injection
  • Routing & Guards: Lazy loading, route guards and nested routes
  • HTTP & REST: HttpClient, interceptors, error handling
  • Testing: Unit tests with Jasmine & Karma, basic e2e
  • Performance: Change detection, OnPush, optimization techniques
  • Build & Deploy: CLI, environment setup, production build

Projects you will build

  1. Task manager app with authentication, CRUD & filters
  2. Data dashboard with charts and server-side pagination
  3. Capstone: E-commerce mini-app (product catalog, cart, checkout mock)

Who should join

Fresh graduates, frontend developers looking to upskill, backend developers transitioning to full stack, and working professionals preparing for interviews.

Outcomes

  • Understand RxJS patterns and state management
  • Ship projects suitable for your portfolio & interviews
